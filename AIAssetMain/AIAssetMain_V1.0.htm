How it works

From Defense-Grade Research to Everyday AI Accuracy

DocuGraph is built on methodologies refined through Department of Defense–sponsored research in document understanding, semantic modeling, and AI explainability. The same technology that enables mission-critical compliance, intelligence analysis, and operational decision-making is now available for commercial, healthcare, and regulated industry use.

Step 1 – Upload Drag-and-drop PDFs, Word, Excel, or images. Secure, fast, and ready to process.
Step 2 – Understand AI reads text, layout, and visuals together for maximum context.
Step 3 – Label Entities, sections, and relationships are tagged automatically—no manual work.
Step 4 – Link Content is structured into a Semantic Knowledge Graph with full traceability.
Step 5 – Act Query, integrate, and get AI answers you can trust—instantly.

Why It Works Better

  • Defense-grade precision: Proven in DoD compliance and intelligence workflows
  • Explainable results: Every answer traceable to its source document
  • No manual labeling: Saves weeks of preparation time
  • Scales effortlessly: From a handful of files to enterprise-scale archives
